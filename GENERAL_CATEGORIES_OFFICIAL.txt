CORRECT CATEGORIES (As per official format):

01. SERIOUS CRIMES COMMITTED
    - Homicides, murders

    - Recovery of arms, ammunition, explosives
    - Other serious criminal activities

02. RAPE, SEXUAL ASSAULT & CHILD ABUSE
    - Rape cases
    - Sexual assault
    - Sexual abuse
    - Child abuse
    - Molestation
    - Indecent behavior

03. FATAL ACCIDENTS
    - Road accidents with fatalities
    - Fatal traffic accidents
    - Deaths due to accidents

04. POLICE OFFICERS/VEHICLES INVOLVED IN ROAD ACCIDENTS AND DAMAGES TO SRI LANKA POLICE PROPERTY
    - Police officers involved in accidents
    - Police vehicles in accidents
    - Damages to police property
    - Police-related accident incidents

05. FINDING OF DEAD BODIES UNDER SUSPICIOUS CIRCUMSTANCES
    - Unidentified dead bodies
    - Suspicious deaths
    - Bodies found under suspicious circumstances
    - Unexplained deaths

06. SERIOUS INJURY/ ILLNESSES/ DEATHS OF POLICE OFFICERS
    - Police officers seriously injured
    - Police officers with serious illnesses
    - Deaths of police officers
    - SGOO (Senior Gazetted Officer) related incidents
    - Hospital admissions of police officers

07. DETECT OF NARCOTIC AND ILLEGAL LIQUOR
    - Narcotic detections
    - Drug seizures (heroin, cocaine, cannabis, etc.)
    - Illegal liquor detections

    - Army personnel arrests
    - Navy personnel arrests
    - Air Force personnel arrests
    - Military personnel arrests
    - Tri-forces related incidents

09. OTHER MATTERS
    - Incidents not fitting other categories
    - Miscellaneous police matters
    - General incidents
    - Default category for uncategorized items

10. [RESERVED]
    - Reserved for future use
    - Special categories as needed

USAGE
================================================================================

The system automatically categorizes incidents based on keywords in the
incident body and summary. Priority is given to specific categories (02-08)
before defaulting to category 01 (Serious Crimes) or 09 (Other Matters).

Example:
- Incident with "rape" → Category 02
- Incident with "fatal accident" → Category 03
- Incident with "narcotic" → Category 07
- Incident with "army" → Category 08
- Incident with "theft" → Category 01
- Incident with no specific keywords → Category 09
