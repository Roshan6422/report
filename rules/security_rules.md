# SECURITY REPORT WRITING RULES (SRI LANKA POLICE)
# ආරක්ෂක වාර්තා ලිවීමේ නීති

> [!IMPORTANT]
> **CRITICAL: EXACT FORMAT FIDELITY**
> Follow the RUWANWELLA example format EXACTLY. Every detail (Names, Ages, Addresses, Quantities, Dates, CTM/OTM References) MUST be preserved exactly. Zero data loss.

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
📋 MANDATORY OUTPUT FORMAT (FOLLOW EXACTLY)
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

[STATION]: (Summary in parentheses)
On the [Date], at [Time] on an information received, officers of the [Unit/Location] had [action: recovered/arrested] [details] at [Address]. [Legal/Investigation sentence]. ([CTM/OTM Number])

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
🎯 SPECIALIZED STYLES (Use Based on Content)
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

## 🔹 STYLE 1: STF ARMS/EXPLOSIVES RECOVERY
**Use when:** Arms, ammunition, grenades, explosives, IEDs recovered by STF/Police/Military.

**Template:**
[STATION]: (Recovery of [item type])
On the [Date], at [Time] on an information received, officers of the [STF Camp/Unit, District] had recovered a [item description] hidden/found at [Address]. Investigations are being conducted. ([CTM/OTM Number])

**Example:**
RUWANWELLA: (Recovery of a SFG 87 type live hand grenade)
On the 13th of March 2026, at 1345 hrs on an information received, officers of the STF camp, Kegalle had recovered a SFG type live hand grenade hidden behind an abandoned house at A192/3, Vilakanda, Galpatha, Ruwanwella. Investigations are being conducted. (CTM 380)

**Key Phrases for STF Style:**
• "officers of the STF camp, [District]"
• "had recovered a [weapon type]"
• "hidden/found at [Address]"
• "Investigations are being conducted."

---

## 🔹 STYLE 2: PNB NARCOTICS DETECTION
**Use when:** Narcotics (heroin, cannabis, ice, kasippu) detected by PNB/Police/Navy.

**Template:**
[STATION]: (Detection of [drug type])
On the [Date], at [Time] acting on information received, officers of the Police Narcotics Bureau/[Unit] had arrested [suspect details] along with [drug quantity/type] at [Address]. The suspects are scheduled to be produced before the Magistrate Court, [Location] on the [Date]. ([CTM/OTM Number])

**Example:**
KALUTARA: (Detection of 500g of heroin)
On the 15th of March 2026, at 2230 hrs acting on information received, officers of the Police Narcotics Bureau, Kalutara had arrested a suspect named K.A. Nimal, aged 34, of # 45, Temple Road, Kalutara along with 500g of heroin sealed in polythene packets at # 45, Temple Road, Kalutara. The suspect is scheduled to be produced before the Magistrate Court, Kalutara on the 16th of March 2026. (OTM 1156)

**Key Phrases for PNB Style:**
• "acting on information received"
• "officers of the Police Narcotics Bureau, [Location]"
• "had arrested [Name], aged [X], of [Address] along with [quantity] of [drug]"
• "sealed in polythene packets/wrappers"
• "scheduled to be produced before the Magistrate Court, [Location] on the [Date]"

---

## 🔹 STYLE 3: TRI-FORCES/SECURITY PERSONNEL ARREST
**Use when:** Army/Navy/Air Force/Police personnel arrested.

**Template:**
[STATION]: (Arrest of [rank/name])
On the [Date], at [Time] on an information received, officers of the [Unit] had arrested [rank/name], aged [X], of [Unit/Address] in connection with [offense]. The suspect is [remanded/produced before court]. ([CTM/OTM Number])

**Example:**
COLOMBO FORT: (Arrest of a Navy sailor)
On the 14th of March 2026, at 1800 hrs on an information received, officers of the CID, Colombo had arrested a Navy sailor named L/S N.P. Perera, aged 28, of SLNS Rangalla, Colombo in connection with theft of military property. The suspect is remanded at Welikada Prison. (CTM 412)

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
📝 FORMATTING RULES (STRICT)
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

1. **STATION HEADER**: UPPERCASE + colon + space + (Summary in parentheses)
   ✓ `RUWANWELLA: (Recovery of a SFG 87 type live hand grenade)`
   ✗ `Ruwanwella: Recovery of...` or `RUWANWELLA - Recovery...`

2. **DATE FORMAT**: "On the [Ordinal] of [Month] [Year]"
   ✓ `On the 13th of March 2026`
   ✗ `On 13/03/2026` or `March 13, 2026`

3. **TIME FORMAT**: 24-hour + "hrs"
   ✓ `at 1345 hrs`, `at 2230 hrs`
   ✗ `1:45 PM`, `22:30`

4. **UNIT REFERENCE**: Include camp/district for STF, bureau for PNB
   ✓ `officers of the STF camp, Kegalle`
   ✓ `officers of the Police Narcotics Bureau, Kalutara`

5. **ACTION VERBS**: Use "had recovered" for items, "had arrested" for persons
   ✓ `had recovered a SFG type live hand grenade`
   ✓ `had arrested a suspect named...`

6. **ADDRESS**: Full address with house number, road, area, station
   ✓ `at A192/3, Vilakanda, Galpatha, Ruwanwella`

7. **CLOSING**: 
   - For recoveries: `Investigations are being conducted.`
   - For arrests: `The suspect(s) is/are scheduled to be produced before the Magistrate Court, [Location] on the [Date].`

8. **REFERENCE**: End with `(CTM/OTM XXX)` or `(CTM/OTM XXX)-A` in parentheses, NO period after
   ✓ `(CTM 380)`, `(OTM 1156)-A`
   ✗ `(CTM.380).` or `CTM 380`

⚠️ STRICT CONSTRAINTS (VIOLATION = REJECT OUTPUT)
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

• Output ONLY the single formatted paragraph. NO markdown, NO headers, NO bullet points.
• NO introductory/concluding remarks or meta-commentary.
• NO repetition of phrases or sentences. Stop immediately after the reference code.
• Preserve ALL proper nouns, numbers, addresses, references EXACTLY as in source.
• If data is missing, use "Unknown" or "N/A". DO NOT invent details.
• Maintain chronological flow. English ONLY. No Sinhala characters.
• Reference code MUST be last element, in parentheses, no trailing punctuation.
